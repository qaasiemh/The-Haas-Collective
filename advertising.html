Advertising

Haas Advertising is a totally integrated ad agency with skills and expertise across all channels. With creative strategists at the helm of the agency you’ll find creativity infused in every aspect of the communication process — from strategy to execution, resulting in unique and innovative solutions to business problems.

It forms part of the Haas Collective, alongside a concept store, an art gallery and a high-end coffee house and roastery, situated in the Design and Innovation Precinct of Cape Town. It was founded with the idea to develop a ‘creative hub’; a space that would attract creative talent and expertise and facilitate the start of creative conversations – with the aid of a little single origin coffee. It’s in this relaxed spirit of creativity that our clients love to participate and interact and in which many big ideas have been born.

Creatively led by Glynn Venter and Francois de Villiers we offer our clients big agency and big brand experience through small agency relationship building and service. After years in the industry we realise that success, in any venture, is totally dependent on the quality of the relationships within it – relationships built on trust, transparency and respect of brand DNA.

Our Values

We believe that there are a few key ingredients in building meaningful partnerships with consumers, clients and suppliers. At the heart of Haas Advertising, you’ll find respect, integrity and good coffee. We are responsible and committed to finding and implementing the best possible solutions and sharing in its success. We help to grow brands by guarding their DNA, while also finding honest creative concepts that affect positive change within our clients’ businesses. And we do so through consistently integrating joy and passion into everything we do.
